How can you help?

Donations

Donations are crucial to our success as a club. While we try to bring the campus together in
a technology centered way, we try to do it without costs to students themselves. This allows
us to stay active as a club, and allows us to represent CNU, and the students within our chapter.
We are always hosting events which can use individual support. Any ammount is appreciated and goes to good use.

How you can donate?

Currently, our donations come in through checks made out to our Club. This is the only official
way that we can accept donations in a way that is transparent to the University. Our faculty
advisor, Dr. Roberto Flores is the one who manages the reciept of these donations.
Once recieved, the funds go directly into an account that the Association for Computing Machinery
has with the university.


If you would like to donate to us by check, then please make the check out to
The Association for Computing Machinery At CNU and memo it with Donation
The check should be mailed to the following address:

1 Avenue of the Arts,
Attn: Roberto Flores,
Newport News, VA, 23606-3072


Thank you for your consideration, and generosity.

We are currently working with the University to set up an online form for donations.
This is a process that takes time, and we will let you know as soon as this is approved
